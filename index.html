About Phoenix

Gauteng's Trusted
Waterproofing Specialists

Based in Weavind Park, Phoenix Waterproofing & Projects delivers durable, professional solutions across Gauteng. We don't just patch problems — we provide lasting protection for your home or business. Every waterproofing job comes with our 5-year warranty, because we stand behind every single job we do.

  • Waterproofing – all types: Liquid membrane, torch-on, epoxy, green roofs
  • General roof repair & roofing: Leak detection, tile & metal repairs, re-roofing
  • Interior & exterior painting: Residential and commercial, all surfaces
  • Gutter repair & installation: Seamless gutters, downpipes, leaf guards
  • Isotherm & ceiling works: Insulation, ceiling board replacement & repairs
  • Barge & fissure boards: Replacement, repair, and painting
