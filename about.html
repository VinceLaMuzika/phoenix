Our Story

Built on Quality. Driven by Commitment.

Two core principles have guided us from the very beginning — and they still do today.

Our Journey

Phoenix Waterproofing & Projects began as a small operation with a simple mission: protect Pretoria homes and businesses properly. As word spread about our quality workmanship and reliable service, our client base grew — and so did our capabilities.

Today we're a fully registered company based in Weavind Park, serving clients across Pretoria with a comprehensive range of waterproofing, roofing, painting, and building services.

Our Commitment

We understand that your property is one of your biggest investments. That's why we treat every project — from a simple gutter repair to a complete roof waterproofing — with the same level of care and professionalism.

Our team stays current on the latest waterproofing technologies and techniques, ensuring you get solutions that genuinely last. Whether it's liquid membrane, torch-on, or specialist coatings — we have the expertise to protect your property for years to come.
